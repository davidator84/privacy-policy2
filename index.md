Privacy Policy – Interval Master

Effective date: 31 May 2026

1. Overview

Interval Master is a mobile application designed to help users practice musical intervals and timing exercises.

This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user data.

2. Data Controller

Developer: David Thomas
Contact: davidator@gmx.de

3. Data Collection

Interval Master does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

No user accounts or registration are required to use the app.

4. In-App Purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases to unlock premium features.

Payments are securely processed via Google Play Billing. The developer does not have access to any payment information such as credit card details.

Google may process payment-related data according to its own privacy policy.

5. Local Storage

The app may store limited data locally on the user’s device, such as:

unlocked premium status
user preferences and settings

This data remains stored exclusively on the device and is not shared externally.

6. Analytics and Tracking

The app does not use analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or any form of user tracking.

No usage data is collected or transmitted.

7. Data Sharing

No personal data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

8. Third-Party Services

The app only uses the following third-party service:

Google Play Services (for app distribution and billing)

These services may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

9. Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored on external servers, user data remains on the device and is protected by the device’s security mechanisms.

10. Children’s Privacy

The app does not specifically target children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.
